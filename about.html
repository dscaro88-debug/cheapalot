About CheapALot

Built around one buying advantage: lower China supply cost.

We are based in Yiwu, Zhejiang, and work with overseas trade buyers looking for low-cost wholesale goods, clearance opportunities, factory overstock and sourcing support.

What we are

A sourcing and wholesale enquiry service—not an automated retail shop.

Our role

CheapALot connects international buying requirements with supply in Yiwu and wider China. The website shows selected trade opportunities, while the larger part of our work starts from a buyer brief: product, quantity, target price, delivery country and timing.

What we help with

  • Clearance stock, factory overstock, tail goods and end-of-line opportunities.
  • Low-cost repeatable wholesale products from China.
  • Supplier search and quotation comparison.
  • Samples, order follow-up and optional pre-shipment inspection.
  • Consolidation and coordination with the agreed logistics provider.

How we communicate price

Website prices are indicative. We reconfirm current supplier, stock condition, quantity, MOQ, packaging, price and lead time in writing before an order. This is especially important for clearance and tail-stock lots, which may change quickly.

Who we serve

Importers, discount retailers, wholesalers, market traders, ecommerce sellers and other business buyers. CheapALot is not intended for personal consumer purchases.

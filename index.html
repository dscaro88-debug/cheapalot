Buyer questions

Before you request a quote

Are the website prices guaranteed?

No. They are indicative trade prices. We confirm live price, stock, MOQ and condition in a written quotation before an order.

Can you find products not shown online?

Yes. Send a photo, product link, specification, quantity and target price for a Yiwu or wider China sourcing search.

Do you sell clearance and factory tail goods?

Yes, when current opportunities are available. These lots can change quickly, so condition, quantity and availability must be reconfirmed.

Can different products be consolidated?

Consolidation can be discussed in the quotation after product volumes, supplier locations and shipping requirements are known.

Is this a consumer shop?

No. CheapALot is for international trade buyers, resellers, importers and business purchasing.

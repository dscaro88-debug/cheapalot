📍 YIWU CHINA — GLOBAL NO.1 WHOLESALE HUB

The World's Small Commodities Capital — Where Every Product Begins

Yiwu International Trade City, Zhejiang, China — the planet's most complete, most up-to-date small commodities distribution center. Every day, millions of products leave here for 200+ countries.

Why Yiwu Matters to Your Business

Located in Zhejiang Province, China, Yiwu International Trade City (义乌国际商贸城) is officially the world's largest small commodities wholesale market — a title recognized by the United Nations, the Chinese State Council, and global trade bodies.

Spread across 4 million m² of selling space and divided into 5 districts (Futian Market), the market hosts over 75,000 booths stocked with more than 1.8 million SKUs. If a product is made in China, you can find it here — usually within the same day, often at factory-floor prices.

What truly sets Yiwu apart is the daily fresh stock turnover: thousands of new SKUs land every week, seasonal lines rotate by the month, and surplus / end-of-line / overstock inventory is liquidated in real time. This is why global buyers — from pound shops in the UK, discount retailers in Europe, dollar stores in the USA, to bazaars in the Middle East and Africa — source their goods here.

What You Can Source in Yiwu

  • 🏠 Household & Kitchen — Cleaning supplies, storage, organizers, decor, plastic goods, kitchen gadgets
  • 👜 Bags, Luggage & Accessories — Handbags, backpacks, wallets, travel gear, fashion accessories
  • 🧸 Toys, Crafts & Stationery — Plush toys, DIY, school supplies, office goods, party items
  • 💄 Beauty & Personal Care — Cosmetics, hair care, hygiene, nail art, travel-size toiletries
  • ⚽ Sports & Outdoor — Camping, fitness, beach, BBQ, garden essentials
  • 👕 Apparel, Footwear & Textiles — Socks, underwear, scarves, hats, workwear, fabrics
  • 🔧 Hardware, Tools & Auto Accessories — Hand tools, electronics, car accessories, lights
  • 🍫 Food, Snacks & Pet Supplies — Confectionery, beverages, pet treats, aquarium goods
  • 🎄 Seasonal & Festive — Christmas, Halloween, Easter, Ramadan, Chinese New Year items

How Goods Leave Yiwu Every Day

Yiwu is the export heart of the Yangtze River Delta. Over 1,800 containers leave the Yiwu port daily — bound for Europe, the Americas, Africa, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia. Direct freight routes connect Yiwu to Ningbo-Zhoushan Port (world's busiest cargo port by tonnage) and the Yiwu–London Yiwu–Madrid China Railway Express freight trains.

Average lead time: 15–25 days to most European & Middle East destinations, 20–30 days to Africa & the Americas.

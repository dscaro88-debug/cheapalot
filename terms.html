Trade terms

Website information is a starting point, not a binding offer.

Every order is governed by the specific written quotation, pro forma invoice or contract accepted for that transaction.

Do not make payment based only on a website listing or informal message.

1. Business use

CheapALot provides wholesale sourcing and trade enquiry services. The website is intended for importers, resellers and other business purchasers, not personal consumer buying.

2. Prices and availability

Website prices, minimum quantities, stock labels and descriptions are indicative. They may change and are subject to written reconfirmation of supplier, quantity, condition, packing, currency, Incoterm, lead time and validity period.

3. Orders and payment

No website form creates an order. An order exists only after the parties accept the applicable quotation, invoice or contract. Payment instructions must be verified against the formal transaction document before funds are sent.

4. Samples, inspection and compliance

The buyer is responsible for explaining the destination market and required standards. Samples, testing, inspection and certification requirements must be agreed before order confirmation. Optional checks reduce risk but do not replace buyer due diligence.

5. Clearance and tail goods

Clearance, overstock and tail goods may be one-off, mixed, unbranded, repacked or available for a limited time. The written quotation should specify known condition and scope. Buyers should request samples or inspection when condition is material.

6. Shipping and import

Shipping cost, insurance, duties, taxes, customs clearance and delivery responsibility depend on the agreed Incoterm and quotation. Estimated transit times are not guarantees.

7. Enquiries and privacy

Information submitted through the website is used to respond to the trade request and operate the related sourcing discussion. Do not submit payment-card data, passwords or other unnecessary sensitive information through website forms.
